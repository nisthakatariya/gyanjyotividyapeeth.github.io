Gyaan Jyotii Vidyapeeth

Gyaan Jyotii Vidyapeeth came into existence in 2000 at B-55 vidya nagar jagatpura with only a few students. The only motto was the betterment of deteriorating condition of studies in jaipur at that time. The time as its habit is ticking regularly and constantly, the sapling has become a beautiful tree. The school has spacious rooms, with an attractive furniture. A variety of activities are there in the school to develop the personality and for healthy and active participation in life.
